# Legal & Tax Operations Toolkit

A practical guide and operational framework for managing legal matters and multi-jurisdiction taxes without enterprise-level firm support.

Built for individuals, families, small LLCs, and anyone navigating:
- **Pro se litigation** (self-represented in court)
- **Multi-country tax compliance** (US, UK, Canada, and others)
- **Multiple LLC/entity management** (including forgotten ones)
- **Family and small-business tax coordination**

---

## Who This Is For

- Self-represented litigants who need to match what law firms do, at 1% of the cost
- Founders and freelancers with entities in multiple states/countries
- Families coordinating taxes across members and jurisdictions
- Anyone who has ever said "I think I still have an LLC in Wyoming"

## What This Is Not

- Legal advice (consult a licensed attorney for your jurisdiction)
- Tax advice (consult a CPA or enrolled agent)
- A substitute for professional counsel on complex matters

This is an **operational playbook** — what tools to use, how to organize, what processes to run, and what questions to ask your professionals.

## Quick Start

1. **Audit your entities** — Use `templates/entity-registry.md` to list every LLC, corp, or entity you've ever formed
2. **Map your jurisdictions** — Read `tax/multi-jurisdiction.md` to understand your filing obligations
3. **Set up your calendar** — Use `tax/annual-checklist.md` to never miss a deadline
4. **Choose your tools** — Read `legal/tools.md` and `tax/tools.md` for what to buy at your budget level
