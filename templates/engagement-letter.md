# Engagement Letter Checklist

Before hiring a lawyer or tax professional, get answers to these questions in writing.

---

## For Legal Counsel

### Scope
- [ ] What specific services are included?
- [ ] What is explicitly excluded?
- [ ] Is this full representation or limited scope?
- [ ] Which courts/jurisdictions are covered?

### Billing
- [ ] What is the hourly rate? (partner vs. associate vs. paralegal)
- [ ] Is there a retainer? How much? Refundable?
- [ ] What is the estimated total cost to resolution?
- [ ] How often will I receive invoices?
- [ ] Are emails and phone calls billed? (At what rate?)
- [ ] Are there additional costs? (Filing fees, court costs, expert witnesses, travel)

### Communication
- [ ] Who is my primary point of contact?
- [ ] What is the expected response time for emails/calls?
- [ ] Will I receive copies of all filings and correspondence?
- [ ] How will you communicate case updates?

### Termination
- [ ] How can I terminate the engagement?
- [ ] What happens to my files if I terminate?
- [ ] Is there a termination fee?
- [ ] What is the procedure for substitution of counsel?

### Conflicts
- [ ] Have you checked for conflicts of interest?
- [ ] Have you ever represented the opposing party or their counsel?
- [ ] Do you have any business relationship with opposing counsel?

---

## For Tax Professionals

### Scope
- [ ] Which returns are included? (Federal, state, foreign)
- [ ] Is FBAR/FATCA filing included?
- [ ] Is entity return filing included? (Which entities?)
- [ ] Is tax planning advice included or just compliance?

### Experience
- [ ] How many clients with similar situations do you serve?
- [ ] Are you experienced with [specific jurisdiction]?
- [ ] What is your experience with international tax treaties?

### Billing
- [ ] Flat fee or hourly? Per return or bundled?
- [ ] What additional charges apply? (Amended returns, IRS correspondence)
- [ ] When is payment due?

### Process
- [ ] What information do you need from me and by when?
- [ ] When will my returns be ready for review?
- [ ] Do you file electronically?
- [ ] Will you represent me if audited? (Additional cost?)

### Liability
- [ ] Do you carry professional liability insurance?
- [ ] What is your policy on errors?
